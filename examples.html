Examples of messages sent by spies

Example 1:

… --- …

In morse code, this means SOS.

SOS is a help signal that stands for "Save Our Ship". It was a morse code alert for distressed ships, however nowadays you would associate it with any danger.

They chose this as a signal due to it being simple, quick, and easily recognizable.
